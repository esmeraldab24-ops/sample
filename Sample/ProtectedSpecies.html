Protected Marine Species

About

Protected marine species are ocean animals that are protected by laws because they may be endangered, threatened, or at risk. According to NOAA Fisheries, protected marine life includes animals such as sea turtles, whales, seals, dolphins, and corals.

Quick Facts

  • NOAA Fisheries require ocean and coastal projects like offshore wind construction, dredging, bridge repairs, and underwater research to follow mitigation, monitoring, and reporting rules when their work may affect protected marine animals.
  • All sea turtles found in U.S. waters are protected under the Endangered Species Act.
  • All marine mammals are protected under the Marine Mammal Protection Act.

Examples of Protected Species

  1. Loggerhead Sea Turtles
  2. Corals
  3. Hawaiian Monk Seals
  4. Bottlenose Dolphins
  5. Sperm Whales
